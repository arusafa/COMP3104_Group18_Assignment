Program Overview
Today, few organizations make any significant plans without
thoroughly understanding the Information Technology (IT) implications.
IT professionals are a vital part of decision-making business teams. 
  -George Brown College answers this need with its Computer Programmer 
  Analyst program. The broad education in programming and IT analysis 
  that it offers can provide you with a stable platform for career growth 
  in the rapidly expanding and ever-changing world of information technology.
